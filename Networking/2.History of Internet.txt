-------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. WHO OWNS THE INTERNET? (Internet Ka Malik Kaun Hai?)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
ANSWER: NOBODY DOES! (Koi bhi ek insaan ya company Internet ki malik nahi hai).

Internet kisi ek single entity ki property nahi hai aur na hi ise koi akele 
control karta hai. Internet asal mein duniya bhar ke lakho chhote-bade private 
aur public Wide-Area Networks (WANs) ka ek "Network of Networks" hai.

* Network Ownership: Inn chhote networks ko alag-alag log, companies, 
  universities, ya governments own aur manage karti hain.
* The Real Definition: Internet ka matlab sirf yeh hai ki inn saare alag-alag 
  networks ko aapas mein RUELE aur PROTOCOLS ke zariye kaise CONNECT kiya gaya hai.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. INTERNET BACKBONE & ISPs (Data Travel Ki Highway)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Duniya bhar mein data itni fast speed se travel kaise karta hai? Uske peeche 
do main components kaam karte hain:

* Internet Backbone (Data Ki Main Super-Highway):
  Yeh Ultra-High-Speed, giant fiber-optic cable networks hote hain jo continents 
  aur samundar ke niche se guzarte hain aur heavy internet traffic ko carry karte hain.
  - Providers: Inhe AT&T, IBM, Tata Communications, Verizon jaisi badi Tier-1 
    telecom companies manage karti hain.

* Internet Service Provider (ISP):
  Yeh woh local companies hoti hain jo aam logon ya offices ko Internet Backbone 
  se connect karne ki service diti hain.
  - Examples: Jio, Airtel, Vi, Comcast, AT&T.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. INTERNET CONNECTION TECHNOLOGIES (Ghar Tak Internet Kaise Aata Hai?)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ghar ya office ke computers ko Internet se connect karne ke liye alag-alag 
technologies use hoti hain:

* 1. Dial-up / Phone Modem (Purana Zamana):
  - Function: Yeh computer ke digital data (0s aur 1s) ko Analog Audio Signals 
    mein badalta hai taaki data normal landline telephone cables par travel kar sake. 
    Destination par doosra modem use wapas digital data mein convert karta hai.
  - Speed: Bohot slow (KB/s speed).

* 2. Digital Subscriber Line (DSL):
  - Function: Yeh bhi regular copper phone lines ka hi use karta hai, lekin 
    analog ki jagah direct DIGITAL DATA transfer karta hai.
  - Advantage: Landline phone aur Internet dono ek sath chal sakte hain.

* 3. Cable Modem:
  - Function: Yeh usi Coaxial Cable line ka use karta hai jis par aapke ghar 
    par Cable TV signals aate hain.
  - Speed: DSL se zyada fast aur reliable hoti hai.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. BROADBAND & BANDWIDTH DYNAMICS (Upload vs Download)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Broadband Definition:
  High-speed internet connection jisme data transfer speed traditionally 
  128 Kbps (Bits per second) se kaafi tez hoti hai. Modern standard mein 
  DSL, Cable, Fiber-Optics, aur 5G ko Broadband mana jata hai.

* Asymmetric Speed Factor (Upload vs Download):
  Broadband connections mein Download speed aur Upload speed hamesha SAME 
  nahi hoti:
  - Download Speed (Tez hoti hai): Internet se data apne device par lana 
    (jaise YouTube video dekhna ya game download karna).
  - Upload Speed (Thodi kam hoti hai): Apne device se data Internet par bhejna 
    (jaise Instagram par photo daalna ya email bhejna).
  - Reason: Normal users download 90% zyada karte hain aur upload kam, 
    isliye ISPs download bandwidth zyada allocate karti hain.
